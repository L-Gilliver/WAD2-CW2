A LITTLE ABOUT US...

Here at West Coast, we have been proudly serving the people of Glasgow our quality dishes for almost a decade! Located on St Vincent Street in Glasgow city centre, we are only a short walk from Glasgow Queen Street Train Station so why not come along for a bite to eat!

We use the finest, locally sourced produced where we can for all of our dishes, that our chefs bring to life using bold flavours and rustic presentation to give that fresh homemade feel.

Our menu changes regularly, allowing us to use the tastiest local produce in season, but also keep an eye out for our monthly regional night menu! Every month on the 18th, we host a special regional night, sampling a different countries cuisine for that nights menu.
